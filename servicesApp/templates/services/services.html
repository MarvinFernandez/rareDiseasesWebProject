Projects 2 Rare Diseases

Patients affected by this type of disease see altered both their physical and mental capacities, influencing their sensory and behavioral capacities. The main problems that patients have to face are multiple, from diagnosis to treatment. Because they are rare diseases, resources (funding) for research on these are often scarce. For this reason, knowing all the existing resources can be helpful for researchers, practitioners and patients.
