Rare Diseases Platform

Despite the wide range of diseases that are classified as Rare Diseases (RDs), they have classically received few resources to investigate these pathologies. As a consequence of the lack of information for the diagnosis and treatment of RDs, patients often receive 2-3 misdiagnoses, requiring visits to different doctors and specialists to converge on a lasting diagnosis. Identification and diagnosis takes an average of 5 years, requiring at least 10 years in 1 in 5 cases to obtain a correct diagnosis.
Patients affected by this type of disease see altered both their physical and mental capacities, influencing their sensory and behavioral capacities. In certain cases, life expectancy is not limited although, most affected patients deal with the disease throughout their lives. Early diagnosis improves medical care helping provide a better quality of life and extend their life expectancy.
To improve the prognosis of rare diseases, it is necessary to optimize the time of diagnosis. Therefore, it can have a very positive impact to have a platform where all the symptoms and signs of each rare disease are found, as well as all the new updated information, serving both researchers, doctors, and patients.

definition Rare Diseases

Those that affect a small number of people receive the name of Rare Diseases (RDs) (in Europe it is defined as less than 1 in every 2000 citizens). The number of different RDs existing is estimated between 5000 and 7000, and affect 6-8% of the world population.
